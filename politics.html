Political consulting projects

Norte de Santander 2019 Regional Election


Obtaining 13% more votes for Centro Democrático party than in previous election.

Campaign involvement
Coordinated the campaign strategy for the governor election in Colombia’s Norte de Santander with a candidate of right wing Centro Democrático party, which was under deep scrutiny nation wide due to alleged witness tampering by its leader and founder, former Colombia´s president Álvaro Uribe. Inspite these and a considerable surge of both outsider centrist candidates and multiparty coalitions country wide, still achieved twice as much votes for the party than in 2015 election by presenting the candidate as autonomus, convey to the electorate that inspite of representing Centro Democrático, he had his own set of.

Role
Account manager on site, strategist, data analysis, message design, strategy implementation, grassroots campaign and media planning.

Guatemala 2019 Presidential Election


Second round win by 16% margin for VAMOS candidate.

Campaign involvement
Assisted the planning for the second round of the presidential election. After attaining the second place in the first round with an outsider candidate from VAMOS party, 10% behind the frontrunner establishment candidate from UNE party, the campaign analyzed data in order to seek the endorsement of the "defeated" candidates that could attract more votes. Thanks to these, it was possible to make a successful call to the useful vote, offering a radical change from the outgoing administration, which had no shortage of corruption scandals, as well as lack of good results in sensitive areas like mass migration, insecurity and poverty reduction.

Role
Data analysis, message design, BTL tactics.
This plot shows how, after arriving second by a 10% loss in the first round, it was possible to overcome the result with a 16% win in the second round.

Panama 2019 Presidential Election


10% growth in voting intention for Cambio Democrático's candidate and erosion of frontrunner candidate's lead.

Campaign involvement
Involved in crunching the numbers to identify voter niches to target. For instance, it was found that the VP candidate that joined the ticket of the advised campaign had higher reach than the presidential candidate himself, so one of the data based decisions by the campaign was to boost the image of the VP candidate, specially in the most marginalized zones of the country. Eventhough it wasn't enough to win the election, thanks to the data analysis, it was achieved to increase significantly the voting intent, as well as weaken the frontrunner.

Role
Junior strategist, data analysis, strategy and message design.
This plot shows how the front runner's advantage began a downwards tendency at the same time of a growing tendency in the advised candidate.
